Tesla

is an American company specialized in the production of auto electricity, photovoltaic panels and energy storage systems. It is called so in honor of the well-known inventor Nikola Tesla.

Tesla Inc.

The goal of the house is to accelerate the transition of the world to the use of sources renewable. CEO Elon Musk envisions tesla as an independent technology company, whose purpose is to offer cars electricity at affordable prices to the average consumer to promote the use of renewable sources and non-polluting. In 2016 Tesla presented its economy model, the tesla model 3 with over 300 thousand orders after a week, the highlight of this vehicle was the very affordable price.

Autopilot. autopilot.

Tesla cars come standard with advanced hardware that offers Autopilot functions and autonomous driving at maximum potential via software updates designed to improve functionality over time. This hardware allows the vehicle to proceed for about 60 seconds completely autonomous, thanks to eight cameras and 12 sensors the car controls the surrounding area allowing safe circulation.

Neural networks.

Tesla networks learn in real time the most complicated and diverse scenarios in the world, repeatedly , through 1 million vehicles. A complete structure of Autopilot neural networks involves 48 networks that take 70,000 hours of GPU for training. Together they produce 1,000 distinct tensors (predictions) for each time interval.
